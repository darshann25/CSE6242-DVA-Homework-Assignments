Average node degree is the cumulative sum of all degree values divided by the number of nodes present in the graph. For TheMovieDB dataset, average degree is 1.683.
Diameter of the graph or network diameter is the maximum distance between any pair of nodes in the graph. For TheMovieDB dataset, network diameter is 9.
Average Path Length is the average distance between every pair of nodes. For TheMovieDB dataset, average path length is 2.61.
